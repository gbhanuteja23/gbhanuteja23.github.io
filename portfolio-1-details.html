AR Space Battles 2

Project information

  • Category: AR Game
  • Client: Made for Self Publishing on Google Play
  • Project date: 31st January, 2021
  • Project URL: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.gBhanuteja.aRSpaceBattlesTwo&hl=en&gl=US

Play as Protector of Galaxies to save your people from Enemy spaceships destroying your planet. Augmented Reality based game using custom logic simulating AR SDK so you can play this even if your phone doesn't support ARCore Device.
